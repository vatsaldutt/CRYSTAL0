# Crystal
Crystal OS
This smart AI Crystal has the potential to do anything, from talking to hacking.
Me, Vatsal Dutt, has been working on this for about 2 years and this AI has evolved drastically.
My vision for Crystal is to make it the world's best AI possible. To make it user friendly. Take out the misconception from people that artificial intelligence will take over the world.
And what I say about the people who think that is, a washing machine can't go be an opera singer, it has to like the taste of soap and it has to wash clothes. And that's what Crystal is going to do too. It will do anything she can do to help you and only this AI can do this without saying "Sorry, I can't help you with that" or "Sorry, I don't understand."
